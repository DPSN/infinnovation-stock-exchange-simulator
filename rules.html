Rules

Infi-Invest/Infinnovation16 as of 05/10/16
  • We are presenting before you a very unique simulation game this year at Infinnovation.
    "Infi-Invest"
  • No. of participants: 2 per school
  • It is a simulation game wherein, the representatives invest virtual money given to them in listed shares.
  • The panel consists of students of the host school.
  • The timings would be 9:30-11:30 AM. (offstage)
  • The updates will be given at 12:30 PM on stage. Few updates will also be given during the offstage session.
  • The transactions will be based on Day Trading.
  • All the participants will be accompanied by a broker (host school) who will record the transactions and bring it to the notice of the panel. Simultaneous update of the profile will be done.
  • The investment can only be done on the 40-50 selected listed diversified stocks of BSE and NSE.
  • The back stage event will be followed by the on stage session. The school with maximum profit after the updates(onstage) provided, wins the competition.
Infi-Invest/Infinnovation16 as of 05/10/16
  • INR 10, 00,000 will be given as the virtual capital.
  • Credit facility will be provided @6% on the principal value.
  • Trade commission fees will be charged @INR 10 per transaction.
  • The regulation and speculation of stock prices will be done through this app.(It represents simulated stock exchange and does not reflect the values of a real stock market).
  • The prices of the securities invested in will be provided in the app itself.
  • The prices of the securities will be based on the general trend of the company.
  • The fluctuations on the event day will be regulated by the panel with respect to an imaginary economic situation.
  • The participants must have a minimum of 75% of their account balances invested in securities before the onstage event begins.
  • The updates provided on stage will affect the prices of the stocks held by the participants. The updates will be provided by the panel sector wise, after which the school with the highest portfolio wins.
